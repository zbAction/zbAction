Step 1

Find a modification you want to add. Make sure the modification is safe to use as some modifications may cause harm to your board.

Step 2

Log in to zbAction and then go to the account manager. You can go to the account manager by clicking the link at the top of the page or by clicking here.

Step 3

Click the "Add New Modification" link and follow the instructions.

Step 4

Add the modification's code to your board template. We highly recommend you put the code for all modifications directly below the zbAction code.

Step 5

There is no step 5. Congratulations! You have just added a zbAction modification to your board.
